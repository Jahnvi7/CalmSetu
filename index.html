What's inside

CalmSetu 🌸 is your youth-friendly space for mental wellness. In today's busy world, stress is common, but sharing feelings isn't always easy. That's where CalmSetu steps in—an AI- powered buddy to check your mood 😊, share emotions, and get quick tips like motivation, mindfulness 🧘, and calming exercises. Simple, warm, and stigma-free ❤️.

About us

We are passionate students and tech enthusiasts who care deeply about mental wellness. CalmSetu blends technology + empathy to create a safe companion that listens, encourages, and grows with you. Our mission: make mental health support accessible, friendly, and judgment-free 💙.
