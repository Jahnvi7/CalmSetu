What’s inside

CalmSetu 🌸 is your youth-friendly space for mental wellness. In today’s busy world stress is common, but sharing feelings isn’t always easy. That’s where CalmSetu steps in—an AI- powered buddy to check your mood 😊, share emotions, and get quick tips like motivation, mindfulness 🧘, and calming exercises. Simple, warm, and stigma-free ❤️.

About us

We are a team of passionate students and tech enthusiasts who care deeply about mental wellness. The idea for CalmSetu came from our own experiences and what we saw around us—friends, classmates, and young people silently struggling with stress but not knowing where to turn.
So, we decided to create something different: a platform that blends technology + empathy. With CalmSetu, we’re not just building an app—we’re building a safe companion that listens, encourages, and grows with you. Our mission is simple: to make mental health support accessible, friendly, and judgment-free for everyone. 💙
